Application Process

Five clear steps.

A straightforward process designed to help us understand your readiness, potential, and how BFI support can shape your next chapter.

01

Choose a programme

Select the scholarship path that matches your current academic stage.

02

Prepare documents

Gather materials that help us understand your readiness, history, and need.

03

Complete the application

Provide clear, honest information about your goals, performance, and background.

04

Submit for review

Your application is screened against programme requirements and fit.

05

Receive a decision

Shortlisted applicants move forward and selected scholars are notified.

Before You Apply

Prepare a strong application packet.

A thoughtful application helps us understand both need and potential. Bring your best picture of who you are and what support would unlock for you.

  • Academic records or recent performance evidence
  • Personal statement or motivation details
  • Relevant school or programme information
  • Supporting documents requested for your track
  • Accurate contact information for follow-up
FAQ

Answers for applicants.

Requirements vary by scholarship stage, but applicants should be ready with school records, personal statements, and any requested supporting materials.

Selections consider need, promise, readiness, and how effectively BFI support can shape the next stage of a scholar's journey.

Coverage depends on the programme and may include tuition, tutorials, mentoring, educational resources, or application support.

In most cases it is better to apply to the pathway that best fits your stage. If you are unsure, contact BFI for direction before submitting.

BFI's application process is an access pathway — there is no application fee to apply for any scholarship.
