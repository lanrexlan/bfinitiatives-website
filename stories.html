From Agbeye to Global Recognition

An electrical engineering graduate from OAU whose research passion led to multiple prestigious scholarships including King Fahd and Erasmus Mundus awards, now pursuing a PhD in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Florida.

BFI's mentorship program stood out for me, especially the guidance in refining my research goals and graduate school documents.

Opeyemi Sodiq Owolabi
Background

Opeyemi grew up in Agbeye, Osun State, developed an early research interest in control systems, and graduated from Obafemi Awolowo University in Electronic and Electrical Engineering.

Challenge

His biggest hurdle was presenting his research direction clearly enough in his statement of purpose and interviews for international admissions committees.

BFI's Role

BFI mentors helped him restructure his statement of purpose, refine his materials, and practice for conversations with potential supervisors.

Journey

He prepared early, secured recommendations, sought fee waivers, cold-emailed supervisors, and stayed rigorous through multiple revisions.

Key achievements

  • King Fahd Scholarship recipient
  • Erasmus Mundus Scholarship recipient
  • Multiple R1 university admissions
  • PhD admission with full funding at University of Florida

Milestones

  • 2020-2024: Undergraduate studies at OAU
  • 2023: Research publications in control systems
  • 2024: Joined BFI mentorship program
  • 2024: Secured multiple scholarship offers

Transforming second class to global success

Babatunde worried that a second-class upper degree in chemical engineering would prevent him from earning funded placements. BFI mentorship helped him reframe his profile around research experience, technical strength, and narrative clarity.

The meetings were detailed, and you can see the mentors pouring themselves out with compassion to help and see the mentees' breakthrough.

Babatunde Adedeji
The challenge

He needed to overcome assumptions about how his degree classification would be read by top admissions committees.

What changed

BFI supported him with application strategy, narrative framing, and interview preparation.

Achievements

  • Offers from two top US R1 universities
  • One Master's and one PhD offer
  • Full graduate funding secured
  • Current graduate student at Tulane University

Milestones

  • Early 2024: Concern about competitiveness
  • Mid 2024: Joined BFI mentorship
  • Late 2024: Secured R1 offers

Not all actions may lead to success, but there is no success without an action.

BFI's role
