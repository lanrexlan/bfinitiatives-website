What To Expect

A practical, flexible commitment.

BFI mentoring is designed for busy professionals and academics. The goal is consistency, not burnout.

  • Typical commitment of 1-2 hours per week
  • Remote-friendly mentoring and document review
  • Support on applications, interviews, and academic planning
  • Opportunity to contribute to workshops and review sessions
