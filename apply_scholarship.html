Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our scholarship programs and application process.

What documents do I need to apply?

Required documents typically include academic transcripts, proof of identity, recommendation letters, and a personal statement. Specific requirements vary by program level and are detailed on each application page.

How are scholarship recipients selected?

Our selection committee evaluates applications based on academic merit, financial need, personal character, and potential for future success. The specific weighting of these factors varies by program level.

What does the scholarship cover?

Scholarships may cover tuition fees, books, educational materials, and in some cases, living stipends. The exact coverage depends on the specific program and funding available. Full details are provided to successful applicants.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

You may only apply for one scholarship category at a time, corresponding to your current educational level. If your educational status changes, you may apply for a different scholarship category in the future.

Is there an application fee?

No, there is no application fee for any of our scholarship programs. Bold Footprint Initiatives is committed to making education accessible to all qualified candidates regardless of financial circumstances.
