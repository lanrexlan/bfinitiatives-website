Why support matters

Every contribution expands access.

When you support BFI, you help fund scholars, strengthen mentorship, and widen the network around educational opportunity.

Ways To Support

Donate, volunteer, or partner.

The original support page already covered all three paths. This redesign keeps that scope but gives each route more clarity.

Donate

Fund access directly

Your contribution can help cover educational costs, mentoring infrastructure, and key scholar support moments.

  • Scholarship and tutorial support
  • Application guidance resources
  • Operational support that keeps the pipeline moving
Volunteer

Give your time and expertise

Volunteers strengthen BFI through mentoring, reviews, coordination help, and professional insight.

  • Remote-friendly contributions
  • Mentoring and scholar support
  • Programme and event assistance
Partner

Build long-term impact together

Organisations and aligned communities can partner with BFI to scale scholar outcomes and opportunity pathways.

  • Strategic programme support
  • Resource and opportunity sharing
  • Institutional collaboration
