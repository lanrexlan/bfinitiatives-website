Our Mission

A pipeline that turns support into scholar outcomes.

We combine funding, mentorship, accountability, and opportunity mapping so scholars don't just stay in school — they rise into leadership.

BFI equips high-potential Nigerian scholars with the tools and guidance they need to access globally competitive academic and professional careers.

Designed for scholars, mentors, partners, and grantmakers who want confidence, credibility, and long-term educational impact.
01

Identify

We find high-potential Nigerian students with demonstrated academic promise and drive.

02

Fund & Equip

Comprehensive scholarships covering tuition, materials, accommodation, and living expenses.

03

Mentor & Guide

One-on-one mentorship from accomplished academics and professionals across 9+ disciplines.

04

Launch

Application refinement, interview prep, and placement into globally competitive programs.

Our Approach

How we create impact

Access to education, mentorship, and community support creates the foundation for lifelong success.

Financial Support

Comprehensive scholarships covering tuition, books, accommodation, and living expenses for qualifying Nigerian students.

  • Financial barriers removed
  • Reduced dropout rates
  • Improved academic focus

Knowledge Transfer

One-on-one mentorship from Nigerian industry professionals and academic experts tailored to each scholar's trajectory.

  • Career preparation
  • Personal development
  • Leadership skills

Community Building

A supportive network of peers, alumni, and professionals that extends well beyond graduation into lasting careers.

  • Peer support systems
  • Professional networking
  • Lifelong connections
