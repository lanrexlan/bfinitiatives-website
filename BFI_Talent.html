BFI Talent of the Year Award

A prestigious national recognition for exceptional achievement in academic excellence and professional development among Nigerian students.

Nominate for Award

About the Award

The BFI Talent of the Year Award is our premier recognition program established to celebrate outstanding Nigerian students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance, leadership abilities, and commitment to community service.

This annual award identifies and showcases individuals who not only excel in their studies but also demonstrate the potential to become future leaders and change-makers in their fields and communities.

Recognition

National recognition through our network of educational institutions and corporate partners across Nigeria.

Financial Award

Recipients receive a financial grant to support their educational journey and professional development.

Networking

Access to BFI's extensive network of professionals, mentors, and alumni for career guidance and opportunities.

Excellence Standards

Selection Criteria

Our rigorous selection process evaluates candidates across four key dimensions to identify truly exceptional talent.

Academic Excellence

  • 🎓 Minimum GPA of 4.5 or equivalent academic standing
  • 📋 Strong academic recommendations from faculty
  • 📚 Research publications or academic contributions

Leadership & Innovation

  • 👥 Demonstrated leadership roles in academic or extracurricular activities
  • 💡 Innovative project experience or creative solutions
  • 🎯 Problem-solving capabilities with tangible outcomes

Community Impact

  • 🤝 Volunteer work with measurable community benefits
  • ❤️ Community service initiatives or programs
  • 🌟 Social impact projects addressing local challenges

Professional Experience

  • 💼 Internship performance and recommendations
  • 📈 Work experience quality and relevance to field
  • 🏆 Professional achievements and recognitions

Past Winners

Meet the exceptional individuals who have been recognized as BFI Talent of the Year for their outstanding achievements.

2023

Adewale Akinwale

Electrical Engineering

Adewale Akinwale is a young chap whose dedication and brilliance have set him apart as an academic star. He aced his WASSCE with seven distinctions, including A1 in General Mathematics, Further Mathematics, and Biology, showcasing his exceptional aptitude and determination.

His remarkable performance is a testament to his hard work, focus, and potential to achieve even greater feats in the future. As the 2023 BFI Talent of the Year, Adewale continues to inspire other students with his commitment to academic excellence.

2022

Tijani AbdulHafiz

BSc Chemical Engineering

Abdulafeez Tijani is a first-class graduate of Chemical Engineering from Obafemi Awolowo University, whose exceptional academic journey reflects both dedication and impactful contributions.

He has demonstrated remarkable leadership through his mentorship of peers, promoting diversity and fostering inclusivity in STEM fields. His research pursuits include cutting-edge studies on sustainable energy systems, aligning with global goals for environmental sustainability.

As our 2022 winner, Tijani continues to exemplify how academic excellence can be leveraged for meaningful societal impact.

02 / 03
2021

Alabere Hafsat

MRes Parasite Control, PhD Biomedical Science (in view)

Hafsat Alabere holds a first-class degree in Microbiology from the Federal University of Technology Akure and a Master of Research in Parasite Control from Aberystwyth University, UK, supported by the Commonwealth Shared Scholarship.

Currently pursuing a PhD in Biomedical Sciences at West Virginia University School of Medicine, USA, her research focuses on Non-Coding RNA as potential therapeutics to rescue mitochondrial dysfunction in cardiovascular diseases.

Hafsat's groundbreaking contributions, including SARS-CoV-2 inhibitor discovery and advancements in malaria diagnosis, exemplify her commitment to addressing global health challenges through innovative biomedical research.

How to Nominate

The BFI Talent of the Year selection process is transparent and merit-based. Here's how to nominate a deserving candidate:

1

Submission

Complete the online application form with the nominee's academic credentials, portfolio, and personal statement.

2

Evaluation

Applications are reviewed by our selection committee against the four key criteria areas.

3

Selection

Finalists are interviewed, and the winner is selected based on overall excellence and impact.
