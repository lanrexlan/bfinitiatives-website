Nomination Submitted Successfully!

Thank you for nominating an outstanding talent for the BFI Talent of the Year Award. Your nomination has been received and will be carefully reviewed by our selection committee.

What happens next?

  • You will receive a confirmation email with details of your nomination.
  • We will reach out to the nominee to inform them of their nomination and invite them to complete their portion of the application.
  • The selection committee will review all nominations within 30 days.
  • All nominators will be notified of the results once the selection process is complete.

Nomination Guide

How the Nomination Process Works

Understanding the journey from nomination to award recognition.

1

Submit Nomination

Complete the nomination form with details about the nominee and why they deserve recognition. Be specific about their achievements and impact.

2

Nominee Verification

We'll contact the nominee to inform them of their nomination and invite them to provide additional information to complete their application.

3

Selection Process

Our selection committee evaluates all nominations based on the four key criteria: academic excellence, leadership, community impact, and professional experience.
